Goofy Wizard Arena 3D

Third-person wizard chaos. Grab floating mystery blocks, throw ridiculous spells, bounce enemies into trees, blind them with squid ink, and vaporize anyone who takes 5 damage. Last wizard standing wins.

P1: Mouse aims/turns up, down, left, and right. W/S forward-back. A/D sidestep. E fireball. Q special. R swap.
P2: I/K forward-back, J/L turn, O ability, Y swap, U fireball.
Controller default: Left analog stick moves, right analog stick aims up/down/left/right, RT fires Fireball, LT casts Special, X/Square swaps abilities.
Use Controls / Controllers to assign controllers or remap keyboard/controller bindings per player.
2P mode requires one player on controller or both players on separate controllers.
In 2P mode, the screen splits so each player gets their own third-person camera.
First to 10 Kills: everyone respawns, and the first wizard to reach the kill target wins.
Crown Control: hold the glowing crown to score. Castle Defense: attackers break gates and tower while defenders protect shared stocks.
