TRUNKBALL

3v3 trunkball. Three big steps, then pass or shoot. No one can step into the trunk circle. Cars have collisions, and shots only count if they go into the open trunk.

P1: W/S move, A/D turn, Q/E hand/swat, F pass, Space shoot/jump, Left Shift sprint.
Double-tap W/A/S/D to shove that direction. Out of steps + W = forward shove.

P2: I/K move, J/L turn, U/O hand/swat, H pass, Right Shift shoot/jump, Enter sprint.
Double-tap I/J/K/L to shove that direction.

Blue 0 - 0 Red
Controls
P1: W/S, A/D, Q/E, F, Space, Left Shift sprint
P2: I/K, J/L, U/O, H, Right Shift, Enter sprint

Double-tap movement = shove. Out of steps + forward = shove.
Sprint is brief and must recharge.
Hold throw: red → green → red. Green improves aim.
