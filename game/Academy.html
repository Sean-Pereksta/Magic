Goofy Wizard Arena 3D

Third-person wizard chaos. Grab floating mystery blocks, throw ridiculous spells, bounce enemies into trees, blind them with squid ink, and vaporize anyone who takes 10 damage. Last wizard standing wins.

P1: W/S forward-back, A/D turn, Q ability, E fireball. Arrow keys also work.
P2: I/K forward-back, J/L turn, O ability, U fireball.
In 2P mode, the screen splits so each player gets their own third-person camera.
