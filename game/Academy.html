3. Build Custom Stats

Direct stat: Use one column as the value. Example: ITAC %, % Available, Wiper Rate
Ratio stat: Divide one column by another. Example: Consumer Appointments / Consumer Opportunities
Minimum filter: Hide reps who do not meet the minimum volume for a selected stat in the chosen timeframe.
Ranking score: Each stat creates a rank. That rank is multiplied by the weight. Lowest weighted total wins.
For ratio stats, the tool combines totals first, then calculates the rate. Example: SUM(Appointments) / SUM(Opportunities), not average of row-level rates.
