Crabmancer 3D

You are a wizard leading a leash-bound army of 3D crabs. Alcoves now spawn enemies in squads of 3. Clear the whole squad to earn more crabs.

Each alcove has stronger enemies depending on its tier, and squads cannot chase too far away from their alcove.

Twenty rival AI wizards roam the giant world, grow their own crab armies, clear alcoves, and eventually try to hunt you down.

A glowing blue storm slowly shrinks the safe ring. If you or your crabs enter the storm, you take damage. A blue arrow points you back toward safety.

W is always forward. S backs up. A and D turn. Pick upgrades with Q and E.
