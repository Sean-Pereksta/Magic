TRUNKBALL

Step 1: Build your team • Step 2: Pick arena • Step 3: Start

3v3 trunkball. Three big steps, then pass or shoot. No one can step into the trunk circle. Cars have collisions, and shots only count if they settle into the open trunk.

P1: W/S move, A/D turn, Q/E hand/swat, Space jump, F contextual throw, Left Shift sprint.
Double-tap W/A/S/D to shove that direction. Out of steps + W = forward shove.

P2: I/K move, J/L turn, U/O hand/swat, Right Shift jump, H contextual throw, Enter sprint.
Double-tap I/J/K/L to shove that direction.

Team commands: 1 Spread, 2 Cut to Trunk, 3 Come to Ball, 4 Set Screen, 5 Crash Trunk.
