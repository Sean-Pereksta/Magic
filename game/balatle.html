How this multiplayer demo works

Match rules
  1. There are 5 rounds. Rounds 4 and 5 are Boss rounds.
  2. Each round has a 2:00 play timer. If time expires and you’re below target, you’re eliminated.
  3. Once you reach target, you can keep scoring for a bigger total, or click Finish Round to enter Buy.
  4. Buy Phase lasts 0:30 (or less if the match advances early). Buys only affect your run.
  5. Next round starts when all surviving players are Ready (or timeouts force it).
  6. End of Round 5: highest Total among survivors wins. If only one player remains, they win immediately.
