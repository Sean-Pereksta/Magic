Turncraft Controls

Orders persist. Scout through true fog of war, pursue assigned targets, and win the objective selected by the pre-match vote.

Persistent movementChoose Move, then any map square. The unit keeps taking movement steps until it reaches that square or the nearest open square.
Persistent pursuitAttack an enemy to chase it whenever it leaves weapon range. Stop or Hold clears the pursuit.
Fog and scoutingEvery square outside friendly vision is fully dark. Fast scouts and advanced air units reveal farther than workers and basic troops.
Match objectivesAnnihilation destroys bases, Conquest holds three impact zones, and Central Relic requires carrying the relic home three times.
Frontier bunkerFrontier infantry can enter a nearby Bunker. Each garrisoned troop strengthens its weapon; select the Bunker to unload everyone.
Brood creepBrood structures spread purple creep. Brood units regenerate much faster while standing on it, and most Brood structures require it.
Group ordersAfter multi-selecting, tap a destination to assign formation positions or tap an enemy to focus and pursue it.
Projectile impactRanged damage now resolves when the shot visually reaches its target, not when the firing animation begins.
